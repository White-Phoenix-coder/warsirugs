About Comapny

Warsi Rugs International

As a manufacturer and direct exporter of rugs, we take pride in offering high-quality, handcrafted, and beautifully designed pieces that cater to all tastes and preferences. At Warsi Rugs International, we are passionate about bringing beauty, comfort, and style to your home with our exceptional collection of rugs.

Our Mission : Our mission is simple, to provide you with the perfect rug that enhances your living space, whether you're looking for a luxurious statement piece, a cozy accent for your bedroom, or a durable option for high-traffic areas. We believe that a rug is more than just a decoration – it’s an investment in your home’s atmosphere, comfort, and design.
